IDLE FOOTBALL CLUB

Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Idle Football Club ("the app", "we", "us") is a free-to-play football idle tycoon game. This privacy policy explains what information is collected when you use the app, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

By downloading or using Idle Football Club, you agree to the terms of this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Information We Collect

Idle Football Club itself does not directly collect or store any personal information on our servers. All gameplay data (your club name, progress, assets, match results) is stored locally on your device only.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads.
  • Expo (React Native) — the platform the app is built on may collect crash reports and basic analytics to help us improve stability.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads within the app. AdMob may collect and use data including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system, and app usage information
  • Interaction data with advertisements

This data is used to serve personalised or non-personalised advertisements. Google's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

Data Storage

Your game progress, club details, and settings are saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the app, this data is permanently deleted.

Children's Privacy

Idle Football Club is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

In-App Purchases

Idle Football Club does not currently offer in-app purchases. All content is free. Any future purchases would be processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store, and we would not have access to your payment details.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what data is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalised advertising

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, most requests can be fulfilled by simply uninstalling the app (which deletes all local data) and adjusting your device's ad tracking settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
